PQBVoice.ca is an independent media outlet covering Parksville and Qualicum Beach — built on the belief that honest local journalism should be free, fearless, and rooted in the community it serves. We cover the stories that matter to the people who actually live here.

We're not a corporate chain. We don't have advertisers telling us what to bury. We report on what this community needs to know — local issues, local voices, and the stories that too often get ignored because they're inconvenient for someone with a budget.
